# Code & Concepts
This blog is a place where I document my learning journey in computer science.

It’s not meant to be a polished, educational resource for others (though I hope some of the posts might still be useful to you),
but rather a way for me to track what I’ve learned, reflect on concepts, and challenge myself to think more deeply about topics.

## Why I’m Writing This Blog

As someone who is diving into computer science on my own, I find that writing things down helps me better understand complex topics, spot gaps in my knowledge, and solidify what I’ve learned. This blog is a personal space to organize my thoughts, share resources, and sometimes even make sense of mistakes I’ve made along the way.

Feel free to browse through my posts, and thanks for joining me on this journey!
